Knick Knacks

  • Mixing Bowl

  • 1 to 1 Gluten Free Flour
  • 1 egg
  • Melted Butter
  • Rice Milk
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Brown Sugar
  • Blueberries
  1. First pour the flour, baking soda and salt into the mixing bowl
  2. Then in a separate bowl combine the egg, vanilla extract, milk, melted butter into a separate bowl
  3. Next the ingredients go into the mixing bowl, start to mix the batter
  4. Mix thoroughly try to get out all the lumps if possible!
  5. More milk or an extra egg is fine if the batter still feels thick
  6. Fun Fact since you aren't cooking with gluten the pancakes won't toughen with overmixing!
  7. Finally we can get ready to put it in to the pan
  8. Also taking the time to let the batter sit in the refrigerator is recommended 15 minutes for kudos!
