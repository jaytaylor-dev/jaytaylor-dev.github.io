  1. First put the pan to a low-medium heat
  2. Slowly place a small drizzle of oil or the equivalent of butter in the pan.
  3. Gently pour the preferable amount of batter for your pancake.
  4. Then patience is the key, you want the pancake to fully cook on one side before flipping.
  5. Fluffier pancakes come from the less you flip them! So one flip is the charm
  6. Flip it! Let the other side cook completely before plating.
  7. Repeat until the batter is gone or you have enough pancakes.
  8. Also the batter can be refrigerated for later use!
